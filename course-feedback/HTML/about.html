About Us

The Feedback System is a modern platform designed to improve educational quality and create an efficient communication channel within our university. It's a system where students, instructors, and administrators can freely exchange ideas and feedback to enhance the university's educational and service quality.

Our main goal is to foster transparency, accountability, and continuous improvement. By valuing every stakeholder's voice, we believe we can create a better learning environment for everyone.

Our Mission

To enhance the university's educational quality and create an efficient feedback mechanism that involves all stakeholders.

Our Vision

To create a university community where every voice is heard, opinions are valued, and continuous improvement is implemented.
