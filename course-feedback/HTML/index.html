Welcome to the Student-Course Feedback System

This feedback system aims to provide an open and efficient communication channel among all university stakeholders. Students, instructors, and administrators can easily provide feedback, share ideas, and contribute to improving educational quality.

For Students

Provide your comments on courses, instructors, and university services. Your feedback helps improve educational quality.

For Instructors

View feedback from students. Use it to improve your teaching methods and enrich course content.

For Administrators

Analyze overall feedback data. Make data-driven decisions to improve the university's overall performance.
